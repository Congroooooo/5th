Happy 5th monthsarry Jagi

It's been five months — how quickly time slips through our fingers like a whisper in the wind.
Yet, I still remember the very first moment I saw you.
In a crowded gymnasium filled with noise and movement,
your voice rose above it all —
and suddenly, the chaos became calm,
the world paused, and all I could hear was you.
In that instant, my heart knew peace for the first time.

I know I haven't given you grand gifts, nor taken you to the beautiful places you've always dreamed of.
All I have for now is this simple letter,
a piece of my heart wrapped in words —
but I hope it reminds you that you've always had all of me.

I dream of the day I can finally give you everything you truly deserve.
But until that day comes, please stay.
Please hold my hand, as we continue to grow,
not just as lovers, but as teammates,
as soulmates destined to walk this path together.

This letter shouldn't be filled with apologies,
but with celebration — of us,
of how far we've come,
of the love that bloomed between our quiet moments and loud laughter.
Five months of learning, of loving, of believing in each other.
Five months of building something real.
And my heart hopes for so many more months, years, and lifetimes with you.

Let's never give up.
Let's hold on — tightly, gently, bravely.
Happy 5th Monthsary, jagi dear.
I love you more than words can ever hold.

Forever yours,
Your Jagi
